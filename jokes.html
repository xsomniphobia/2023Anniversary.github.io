Sadie's Favourite Jokes

If you're ever feeling a little blue and don't want to reach out to me or if I'm asleep then here's some of the jokes I would tell you to try and cheer you up. I know however much you deny it my jokes do make you smile at least a little bit. I know it's not the same without my comedic timing but just imagine a ba doom ch after al of them

What did the fish say when he swam into a wall?

Dam.

What's a mummy's favorite kind of music?

Wrap

What’s the difference between outlaws and orphans?

At least outlaws are wanted.

Did you hear about the medieval lamp?

It's a knight light.

Don’t challenge Death to a pillow fight

Unless you’re prepared for the reaper cushions.

Do you know the phrase “One man’s trash is another man’s treasure”?

Wonderful saying, horrible way to find out that you were adopted.

Where did Joe go after getting lost on a minefield?

Everywhere.

I threw a boomerang a few years ago.

I now live in constant fear.

Why are orphans bad at poker?

They don’t know what a full house is.

What do you call a sad strawberry?

A blue berry!

Why shouldn’t you tell secrets in a cornfield?

Too many ears.

What kind of cheese isn’t yours?

Nacho cheese
