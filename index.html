I'm not always good with the whole words thing or sometimes I get total tunnel vision and forget to tell you how much you mean to me. But honey you mean everything to me.You are my anything and everything, you are the person who makes me feel safe, you are the love of my life. I want to be in our 80's looking at our pictures from Toronto remmbering all the adventures we had. I love you so much and nothing will ever change that.
